Are you interested in Free Software and the FSFE? Are you going to a Free Software meeting or are you even organising one? There are various reasons why and when you might like to receive information, campaign or promotion material for Free Software and the FSFE. Whatever it might be, we will be happy to send you our printed material for no cost.

You can order our default package or individual quantities and tell us explicitly what kind of information, campaign or promotion material you need or prefer. Feel free to decide for yourself. However, please keep in mind, that for postal reasons, in general we just send packages up to 1kg. If you need more, please ask in advance.
